About us

Empowering the passionate

We exist to give you the best possible bootcamps. Our quality standards are the highest around. We won't let you go out of a bootcamp or even a day without some new conditionings to empower you. So be prepared to use your visual, auditory and kinetic learning skills to the maximum. To offer the best guidance our courses are designed to use the best neurolingistic practises.

In simple words its all about muscle memory and conditioning. Its not easy to make your brain think and act a certain way, so make sure you don't get stuck and we are able to teach you about 8-10 new conditionings a day.

We are focused on beginners, because the beginning is the hardest part of learning anything. The NLP teaching way is especially usefull in this phase of learning.

TeamGenerator

Presentations
Presentations

Theory(meta-logic) makes you recognice patterns in similar skills so it speeds up your learning, but no presentation will be longer than 20 minutes which is close to your maximum auditory attention capacity

Repetition
Repetition

You don't learn a skill by doing something 1 time, you also dont learn by doing it over and over in the same workshop, we created games and practises to keep your brain interested and get you trough the repetition
